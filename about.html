03 : About me

Basic Information

My name is Haonan Qin, a junior player in computer vision (CV). I have graduated from the Telecommunication school of Xidian University and got a bachelor degree in 2018. At present, I'm studying for an academic master degree at Xidian University, majoring in information and communication engineering.

Research Field

My main research direction is geospatial objection detection, aiming at copying with the challenge of detecting small and densely arranged objects in wide-scale remote sensing imagery, which has shown great significance in military and civilian applications nowadays.

Career Planning

I hope to continue my study in the future and pursue a doctorate in the cross field of remote sensing and computer vision. If possible, I would like to try my best to pursue further study in the CV.
